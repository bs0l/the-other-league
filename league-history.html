Current Ruleset
League & Format
  • 10 teams, PPR scoring
  • 13-week regular season
  • 4-team playoffs, 2 rounds, 2 weeks each
  • Rounds 1 & 2: weeks 14–15 and 16–17
  • Non-playoff teams: roster locked during playoffs
Buy-In & Payouts
  • $5 buy-in due August 1st
  • Late payment: miss 5 bonus points week 1 (introduced 2025)
  • 1st place: $35
  • 2nd place: $10
  • 3rd place: $5
Scoring
  • PPR (1 pt per reception)
  • TD passes: 6 pts
  • Forced fumble: 1.5 pts
  • Recovered fumble: 1.5 pts
  • Missed PATs: −0.5 pts
  • 1.5 pts per net punt, 1 pt fair catch, 1 pt inside 20, 1 pt inside 10
  • Blocked punt: −2 pts
Keepers
  • 2 keepers per team, due 1 week before draft
  • Drafted players: kept at original draft round − 1
  • Undrafted players: kept at ADP + 1 round
  • Max 2 seasons kept (introduced 2022)
  • Cannot keep 1st-round picks
  • Custom keeper rounds managed via ESPN platform (2022+)
Draft
  • Snake draft
  • Order set to inverse of prior season standings
  • Trade review enabled (2024+)
Roster
  • 2 FLEX slots (2024+)
  • No IR slots (removed 2025)
  • No punter slot (removed 2025)
  • League winner receives well-wishes from Dan

2025
  • Buy-In League dues due by August 1st. Late payment forfeits 5 bonus points in week 1.
  • Scoring Punters removed from scoring. QBs revert to standard 4-point passing TDs — the 0.5-pts-per-completion rule removed. QBs now score 6 pts per TD pass.
  • Roster Both IR slots removed. Punter roster slot removed.
2024
  • Roster 2nd FLEX slot added. Punters added as a scoreable position.
  • Scoring QBs now earn 0.5 pts per completion. Punter scoring introduced: 1.5 pts per net punt, 1 pt fair catch, 1 pt inside 20, 1 pt inside 10, −2 pts blocked.
  • Draft Draft order set to inverse order of prior season standings. Trade review enabled.
  • Playoffs Non-playoff teams have roster lock during playoffs — no transactions allowed.
2023
  • Buy-In $5 buy-in introduced — first year with any financial stakes.
2022
  • Keepers Custom keeper round rules introduced via ESPN platform: drafted players kept at original round − 1, undrafted at ADP + 1. Max 2 seasons kept. Cannot keep 1st-round picks.
2017
  • Keepers Keepers introduced — 2 per team. Rules predating ESPN's custom keeper platform (pre-2022); keepers were 1st and 2nd round picks.
2015
  • Teams League expanded from 8 → 10 teams (first full 10-team season).
2014 — League Founded
  • Founded League created as "The Other League!" on ESPN with 8 teams.
  • Buy-In No buy-in — bragging rights only.
